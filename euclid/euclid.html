Some Fun Facts About Euclid's Elements:

  1. Euclid created the basis of all geometry starting with only 5 postulates!
  2. Not only did Euclid write flawless, easy to understand proofs, but he was the first person to write mathematical proofs.
  3. Euclid is way cooler than your geometry teacher. He never used two column proofs or a protractor!
  4. Euclid wrote a total of 13 books on Geometry!
  5. The geometry we all know and love today is actually called Euclidean Geometry! Other, less fun, geometries exist, including hyperbolic and elliptic geometry.
  6. Although Euclid was not the first to prove the Pythagorean Theorem, his proof is the best!
  7. Have you seen his beard?

Top 5 Propositions:

  • Proposition 1: Shows how to constuct an equilateral triangle. There's a reason it's number 1!
  • Proposition 4: Proves that two triangles with two equal sides and an equal angle between the sides are congruent. AKA the Side Angle Side Congruence theorem.
  • Proposition 32: Proves that the measure of an exterior angle in a triangle is equal to the sum of the measures of the interior opposite angles, and that the sum of all of the angle measures in a triangle is equal to 180°
  • Proposition 46: Shows how to constuct a square on any straight line
  • Proposition 47: Proves the Pythagorean Theorem. Fun for the whole family!
